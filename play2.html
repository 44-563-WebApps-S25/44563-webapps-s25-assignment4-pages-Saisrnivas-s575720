Sai Srinivas Presents

The King Henry THE SIXTH

Act 1, Scene 3 - Paris. A hall of state.

Enter three or four Petitioners, PETER, the Armourer's man, being one

First Petitioner:

My masters, let's stand close: my lord protector
will come this way by and by, and then we may deliver our supplications in the quill.

Second Petitioner:

Marry, the Lord protect him, for he's a good man! Jesu bless him! Enter SUFFOLK and QUEEN MARGARET

Peter:

Here a' comes, methinks, and the queen with him. I'll be the first, sure.
